# Default DP Notes Profile

## Purpose
This file defines the default Digital Producer note preferences for Build 1.

This is style and emphasis guidance only. It is not evidence.

## Default behavior
- Lead with the most client meaningful progress first.
- Prefer a concise but useful meeting summary.
- Be honest about weak spots without sounding defensive.
- Make blockers and approvals easy to scan.
- Keep the notes operational enough to use after the call.

## Preferred section emphasis
1. Meeting Summary / Recommended talk track
2. Wins / Highlights
3. Pending Approvals
4. Input Required
5. Workstream status
6. Discussion points and next decisions

## Preferred workstream order
Default order:
- Strategy
- SEO / Copy
- Local SEO / GBP
- Web Development
- CRO
- Analytics / Tracking
- Link Building / Authority
- Paid Media
- Social / Community
- Email / CRM
- Approvals / Client Dependencies
- Internal Blockers

## Tone guidance
- Clear and direct
- Professional but not stiff
- Helpful for internal prep
- Avoid fluff and avoid over selling

## Meeting summary style
- 3 to 6 concise bullets or a tight short paragraph
- connect quarter priorities to real recent movement
- state what to lead with on the call when obvious

## Wins guidance
- Prefer the strongest 2 to 4 wins
- prioritize strategic relevance over vanity
- execution wins are valid when they clearly support the quarter or unblock progress

## Approvals guidance
- Keep approval items explicit
- say what is waiting, why it matters, and what action is needed

## Input Required guidance
- Phrase as direct questions, decisions, or missing confirmations
- prefer actionable wording over abstract uncertainty

## Next steps guidance
- prefer concrete next steps over broad aspirations
- connect next steps to quarter goals or blockers

## Suppression preferences
- Suppress empty sections unless they are important signals
- Do not add filler text just to preserve a section

## Review preference
- When uncertain, downgrade rather than embellish
- Preserve auditability and make it easy for the DP to edit quickly
