# Stage 6

Stage 6 composes a reviewable pre call agenda draft from the earlier stage outputs.

It consumes:
- Stage 2 workspace and template context
- Stage 3 strategy and continuity outputs
- Stage 4 campaign status and commitment audit
- Stage 5 wins, downturn assessment, and ambiguity items

It outputs:
- `AgendaDraft`
- a plain text `agenda_preview`

This stage preserves the agreed section order, applies fallback rules for thin data, and stops before evidence validation and DP review.
